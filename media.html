ABOUT ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the national space agency of India

It operates under the Department of Space (DOS) which is directly overseen by the Prime Minister of India. ISRO is the primary agency in India to perform tasks related to space based applications, space exploration and development of related technologies.
